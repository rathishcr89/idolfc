Welcome to IDOL FC

IDOL Football Club is Chennai’s premier free training center, dedicated to nurturing the next generation of football talent. Located in the heart of the city, we provide high-quality coaching and structured programs for children and youth under the age of 19 (U19). Our mission is to combine expert training with a supportive environment, helping young athletes develop their skills, confidence, and passion for the game

Our Mission

To discover, develop, and discipline young football talent in Chennai by delivering elite coaching, modern training methodologies, and holistic life skills. Our academy bridges the gap between grassroots passion and professional excellence. We provide a safe, structured, and inspiring environment where young athletes can thrive.

The IDOL FC Philosophy

Driven by the belief that every young player deserves a proper guidance, Anandan Thangavelu has established this center to provide Chennai's kids with the structured training infrastructure that was previously hard to access locally. Under his leadership, the academy has grown from a grassroots initiative into one of the city's most respected youth development hubs, actively preparing under-19 athletes for professional clubs.

At IDOL FC, we believe that football is more than just a game—it is a teacher of life. While we take pride in developing technically gifted players, our ultimate goal is to build strong character. We instill core values of teamwork, respect, humility, and resilience in every child who steps onto our pitch.

Whether your child is taking their very first kick or aiming for a professional career, we provide the perfect pathway to reach their full potential.
